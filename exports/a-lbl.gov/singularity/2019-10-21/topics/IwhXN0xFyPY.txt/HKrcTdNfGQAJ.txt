Just one more thing to try - there are environment variables for
PYTHONNOUSERSITE and PYTHONUSERBASE, see
https://docs.python.org/3/using/cmdline.html#envvar-PYTHONNOUSERSITE

It looks like if you set them the .local directory (which is the user
site-packages) would be ignored. Also feel free to share our whole chain of
discussion (or the final result) to the list in case someone in the future
hits this problem! I've personally hit it at least 3 times on our cluster,
and it always looks different but comes down to loading the wrong library
against the container (and is fixed by nuking .local one way or another!)
