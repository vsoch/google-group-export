Hi Dave,

It's not exactly a characteristic of the container itself, but the driver
for identifying the container type is now modular and dynamic. At the
moment, the devel branch has the ability to identify 2 different container
formats (e.g. image and directory), but I have plans to have it support
various others. One of the major benefits of this is support for completely
unprivileged containers (for now and the foreseeable future, images can not
be mounted via loop unprivileged).
